Rancheritos Mobile Web App

A mock mobile app designed for users to make online food orders to their local Rancheritos restaurant. A user can Login/Register, browse Rancheritos full menu, add items to their cart and checkout with stripe. Their is also an admin side that receives and displays order details. This is for in store use for employees.
